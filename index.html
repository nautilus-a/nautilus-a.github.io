Sebin Lee

Ph.D. Candidate

School of Computing, KAIST


Google Scholar | GitHub


I am a Ph.D. candidate in Scalable Graphics, Vision, and Robotics (SGVR) Lab at KAIST advised by Prof. Sung-Eui Yoon. My research spans embodied AI, robotics, and 3D vision, aiming to build intelligent systems that perceive, reason, and act in complex environments.

Research Interests

  • Embodied AI and Robot Foundation Models

    vision-language-action models, world models

  • 3D Vision and Scene Generation

    semantic scenes, depth, optical flow

  • Neural/Differentiable Rendering

    dynamic radiance fields, deformable Gaussian splatting
